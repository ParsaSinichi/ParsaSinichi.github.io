<p class="about-lead">Graduate student in Computer Science and AI R&amp;D Engineer working across speech, language, and computer vision.</p>

<section class="about-section" markdown="1">
## Currently

I am specializing in Artificial Intelligence at **Ferdowsi University of Mashhad**, under the supervision of [Dr. Soheila Ashkezari](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ql5JirMAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao).

At [C1Tech](https://c1tech.co/), I develop speech and language technology for Persian voice assistants. My work includes training Farsi speech-to-text models, contributing to an early benchmark for Farsi STT systems, and fine-tuning models such as Qwen3 and Gemma 3 for intent extraction and slot filling.
</section>

<section class="about-section about-research" markdown="1">
## Research

My interests include **computer vision**, **medical image processing**, **deep learning**, **robotics**, and **speech and language technologies**.

Previously, I worked as a research assistant at the [FUM CARE Lab](https://fum-care.com/). During my undergraduate studies, I worked with [Professor Alireza Akbarzadeh Totonchi](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=DhU-W1gAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao), [Dr. Mohamad-Hoseyn Sigari](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=IVhk6cYAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate), and Dr. Malihe Javidi.
</section>
